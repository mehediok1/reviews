### Are there any specific cleaning instructions I should follow for my terracotta duvet cover?

To maintain the vibrant colors and softness of your terracotta duvet cover, it's essential to follow the manufacturer's cleaning instructions. Most duvet covers can be machine washed using cold water and a gentle detergent, and then tumble dried on low heat. By following these basic care guidelines, you can help ensure that your terracotta duvet cover remains in excellent condition for many years to come.
