### What is a Sleever Bar?

A Sleever Bar, also known as a sleeper bar or a sleeper bar for fencing, is a horizontal beam that is placed on the ground and provides stability and support to the fence posts. It is usually made of wood or metal and comes in various lengths and widths to fit the specific fencing requirements. The main purpose of a sleeper bar is to ensure the fence posts remain firmly in place and do not move or shift over time. 


### Key Features to Consider

When selecting a Sleever Bar, consider the following key features to ensure you choose the right one for your needs: 
1) Material - Wood or metal, 2) Length and width - To fit the fence posts, 3) Durability - Resistant to weather and pests, 4) Flexibility - Can be bent for curves and corners, and 5) Installation - Ease of installation and compatibility with other fencing materials.

Before purchasing a Sleever Bar, consider the following factors: 
1) Budget - Determine the price range you are willing to spend, 2) Fencing type - Ensure the sleeper bar is compatible with your fencing type, 3) Soil type - Assess the soil type and drainage to determine the best material for the sleeper bar, and 4) Installation - Consider the ease of installation and the need for professional help, if any.
