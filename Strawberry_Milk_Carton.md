## FAQ


### What are some benefits of drinking strawberry milk?

Strawberry milk offers several health benefits. It can help improve digestion due to the high fiber content in strawberries. Additionally, strawberries are rich in antioxidants, such as vitamin C and anthocyanins, which can help protect the body against damage from free radicals. Furthermore, strawberry milk can provide energy, as it contains a mix of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats. 

The consumption of strawberry milk may also have a positive impact on heart health, as it is low in saturated fat and cholesterol. Moreover, it can support immune function, thanks to the various vitamins and minerals present in strawberries, including vitamin B, manganese, and copper. Lastly, strawberry milk is a great source of calcium, which is essential for maintaining strong bones and teeth. 


### What are the ingredients in strawberry milk?

The ingredients of strawberry milk typically include milk (or a milk alternative), strawberries, sugar, and stabilizer such as xanthan gum to maintain a smooth, creamy consistency. Some brands may also add artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives to their products, which can affect taste and shelf life. 

When purchasing strawberry milk, it is essential to check the ingredient list for any allergens or potential dietary restrictions. Additionally, it is crucial to select a brand that uses high-quality ingredients and minimizes the use of artificial additives to ensure the best taste and nutritional value for consumers. 


### How does the taste of strawberry milk compare to regular milk?

Strawberry milk has a more pronounced and sweeter flavor compared to regular milk, primarily due to the addition of strawberries and sugar. The sweetness and fruitiness of strawberries tend to dominate the taste, while regular milk is relatively neutral and slightly dairy-sweetened. Strawberry milk can be a refreshing beverage and an enjoyable alternative to regular milk for individuals looking for a change in taste or a natural source of sugars. 

The taste of strawberry milk may vary depending on the brand or type of milk used. Some brands may offer a more intensely flavored strawberry milk, while others may offer a milder version with a more pronounced milk taste. To find the best strawberry milk for your taste preferences, it is recommended to sample various brands and flavors. 


### Why is strawberry milk available in cartons?

Strawberry milk, like many other dairy products, is often packaged in cartons for several reasons. Firstly, cartons provide an airtight seal that helps maintain the freshness of the milk. The packaging also protects the product from the outside environment, which can help prevent contamination and preserve the milk's quality. Secondly, cartons are lightweight and can be conveniently stacked for easy storage and transportation. 

Carton packaging is eco-friendly as it requires less material and energy consumption compared to traditional glass bottles. Furthermore, cartons are easily recyclable, making them a more sustainable option for both manufacturers and consumers. In summary, the use of cartons for strawberry milk can help preserve its freshness, ensure its safety, and promote sustainability. 


### Is there any difference between fresh and shelf-stable strawberry milk?

There is a significant difference between fresh and shelf-stable strawberry milk in terms of taste, texture, and shelf life. Fresh strawberry milk is generally made from fresh strawberries and milk and has a thicker, creamier consistency due to the absence of stabilizers. The taste of fresh strawberry milk can be more vibrant and full-bodied compared to shelf-stable versions. 

Shelf-stable strawberry milk, on the other hand, has a longer shelf life because it typically includes stabilizers, such as xanthan gum, and may contain artificial preservatives. While shelf-stable strawberry milk can last up to 12 or even 24 months, its taste can be less appealing to some consumers due to the use of additives and the altered texture. Fresh strawberry milk offers a more natural and authentic taste but requires refrigeration to maintain its freshness. 


### Is there a lactose-free strawberry milk option?

Yes, many brands offer lactose-free strawberry milk, which is suitable for individuals who are lactose-intolerant or prefer dairy-free products. Lactose-free strawberry milk is typically made using a plant-based milk base, such as soy, almond, or oat, and incorporates the same blend of strawberries, sugar, and stabilizers to create a smooth, creamy texture. 

Lactose-free strawberry milk alternatives provide a similar taste and nutritional profile to their dairy-based counterparts. However, it is essential to check the label for any potential allergens and consider the product's calorie, protein, and sugar content to find the best option for personal needs and preferences. Some brands may also use natural sweeteners or alternative flavors in their dairy-free products to cater to various dietary requirements.
