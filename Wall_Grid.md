## Buyer's Guide

A Wall Grid is a versatile decorative piece that adds a modern touch to any interior, particularly in kitchens, dining areas, and living rooms. ### Material

Wall Grids are typically made from metals like stainless steel or aluminum, making them durable and easy to clean. Consider the material's appearance and finish, as these will influence the overall look of your Wall Grid and how it complements your existing decor. Stainless steel often has a sleek and polished appearance, while aluminum may have a matte or brushed finish. 


### Size

The size of a Wall Grid depends on your available space and desired aesthetic. Consider the dimensions of the space where you plan to hang the Wall Grid, as well as the size of the room or area it will serve. If you have a larger space, choose a larger Wall Grid to create a more significant impact. Smaller Wall Grids are suitable for more intimate settings or as an accent piece. 


### Design

Wall Grids come in various designs, including square, rectangular, and custom shapes. Choose a design that aligns with your personal style and the overall design of your space. Custom shapes can be an excellent option if you favor a more unique or personalized look. Consider the pattern and color of the grid, as well as any special features like adjustable arms or built-in lighting. 


### Installation

Most Wall Grids can be installed without professional help using wall anchors and screws provided by the manufacturer. However, some larger or heavier models may require assistance for proper installation. Consider the weight of the Wall Grid when choosing a location for installation and ensure the wall can support the weight of the product. Additionally, consider the type of surface on which the Wall Grid will be hung, as different materials may require different mounting techniques.
