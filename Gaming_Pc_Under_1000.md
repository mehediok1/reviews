### What are the pros and cons of buying a pre-built gaming PC under 1000?

One of the primary advantages of buying a pre-built gaming PC is convenience, as they are ready to use right out of the box. You don't have to worry about compatibility issues or spend time waiting for individual components to arrive. Additionally, pre-built gaming PCs often come with a warranty for peace of mind. 

However, one of the drawbacks of purchasing a pre-built gaming PC is that it may not be customizable to your specific needs or preferences. You are limited to the pre-selected hardware components, which may impact the system's performance and compatibility with certain games. It may also be challenging to upgrade individual components if needed. 


### How can I ensure the longevity of my gaming PC under 1000?

To ensure the longevity of your gaming PC, it is essential to take proper care of the hardware components and make regular updates. This includes cleaning the system, especially the cooling fans, to prevent dust accumulation and overheating. Ensure that the software is up-to-date, and the hardware drivers are compatible with the latest operating system. 

Regular maintenance, such as monitoring the system's temperature and noise levels, is also crucial for keeping your gaming PC running smoothly. Additionally, consider purchasing a high-quality power supply unit (PSU) to ensure stable power delivery, as this may be a potential cause of system instability or reduced performance.
