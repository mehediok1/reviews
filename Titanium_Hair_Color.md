## Buyer's Guide

Are you looking for a new hair color that's vibrant and long-lasting? Titanium hair color might be the perfect option for you. This innovative formula offers a variety of hair colors with a touch of iridescence for that extra sparkle. But before you dive into the world of titanium hair colors, let's take a closer look at the essential features and considerations to help you make the right choice. 


### Understanding Titanium Hair Colors

Titanium hair colors are a unique formulation that combines traditional hair color with special ingredients, such as titanium dioxide, which is responsible for the iridescent effect. This formula offers a wide range of vibrant shades and allows for more precise color customization as compared to traditional hair colors. It also provides excellent coverage for gray hair, while minimizing damage to your hair. 


### Importance of Skin Tone

When selecting a titanium hair color, it's essential to consider your skin tone, as the color can either complement or stand out in stark contrast to it. Cool-toned individuals will generally benefit from cooler shades, such as plums, grays, or blues, while warmer skin tones will look best with golden, auburn, or red hues. 


### Choosing the Right Shade

Titanium hair colors come in a variety of shades, from subtle highlights to bold, vibrant colors. Consider your desired look and the level of intensity you would like to achieve. If you're unsure, consult with a professional colorist who can help guide you to the perfect shade for your hair and style. 


### Maintenance and Care

To keep your titanium hair color looking its best, it's important to follow a proper maintenance routine. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to protect the color and minimize fading. It is also recommended to use a color-protecting hair mask once a week to maintain the vibrancy of the color. It's best to avoid heat styling as much as possible and to use a heat protectant spray when necessary.
