### Storing and Reheating Tips

If you can't finish your Strawberry Cheesecake Cinnamon Rolls in one sitting, you can store or reheat them for future enjoyment. 1. Storing: If you have leftover uncut rolls, wrap them individually in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and store them in an airtight container at room temperature. You can also freeze the individual rolls for up to 2 months. To thaw, let them sit at room temperature for a few hours or warm them in the microwave for 15-20 seconds. 2. Reheating: If you have already cut and served your rolls, reheat them in the microwave for 10-15 seconds or place them in a preheated oven at 350°F (180°C) for 5-10 minutes, until they are warmed through. Be sure not to overheat, as this can make the rolls dry and tough.
