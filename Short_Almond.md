### Roasted vs. Unroasted

Short almonds typically come in two varieties: roasted and unroasted. Roasted almonds have a rich, buttery flavor and are often seasoned with salt or spices. Unroasted almonds, on the other hand, have a slightly bitter taste, but their nutritional value is higher due to the absence of roasting. Consider your personal preference and culinary needs when deciding between these two options. 


### Storage & Shelf Life

To ensure that your short almonds stay fresh for as long as possible, store them in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Unopened packages can be stored at room temperature, but once opened, short almonds should be consumed within a few weeks to maintain their quality.
