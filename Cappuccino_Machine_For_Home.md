### How do I properly maintain and clean a cappuccino machine?

Proper maintenance and cleaning are essential for the longevity and functionality of your home cappuccino machine. After each use, be sure to clean the milk frother, milk container, and steam wand to prevent build-up of milk residue or bacteria. Remove the milk tubes and rinse them under warm water to remove any remaining milk. 

You should also clean the portafilter basket and filter holder regularly to ensure optimal performance. After each brew, rinse the basket and filter holder under warm water, and wipe them down with a damp cloth. For deeper cleaning, it's recommended to use a descaling solution once a month to remove built-up mineral deposits and keep your machine running smoothly. Always refer to your machine's manual or manufacturer's guidelines for specific cleaning and maintenance instructions.
