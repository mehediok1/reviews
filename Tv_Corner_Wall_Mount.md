## Buyer's Guide

When it comes to setting up your TV, a corner wall mount can be a smart and space-efficient solution. In this buyer's guide, we will discuss the important features, considerations, and general advice for choosing the right TV corner wall mount for your home. 


### Factors to Consider When Choosing a TV Corner Wall Mount

1. TV Size: Ensure that the corner wall mount you choose can support the weight and size of your TV. Check the weight capacity and maximum TV size mentioned in the product specifications before making your purchase. Make sure to consider the swivel and tilt functionality if you want the TV to adjust its position. 


### Types of TV Corner Wall Mounts

1. Fixed Corner Wall Mount: If you want a simple and inexpensive solution, this type might be the best choice for you. It typically has no additional movements, only fixing the TV in the corner. 2. Swivel Corner Wall Mount: This type allows your TV to rotate 180 degrees or more to either side, providing better viewing angles and the ability to share the screen with others. 3. Tilt Corner Wall Mount: This mount enables the TV to tilt up and down, adjusting the angle of the screen according to your preference. 


### Installation Tips

1. Accurately measure the distance between the wall and the TV to ensure proper installation. 2. Follow the manufacturer's instructions carefully, as each corner wall mount may require different tools and techniques. 3. Consider using a level to ensure the TV is hanging straight and securely. 4. Make sure to have a helper when installing the mount, as it can be heavy and require steady hands. 5. Double-check all screws and bolts after installation to prevent any accidents. 


### Safety Considerations

1. Always follow the manufacturer's recommendations and safety guidelines. 2. Ensure proper installation to prevent the TV from falling or becoming unstable. 3. Use a stud finder to locate reliable anchor points on the wall for increased security. 4. Consider using an anti-tip bracket for additional safety, especially if you have children or pets at home. 


## FAQ


### What is a TV corner wall mount, and how does it work?

A TV corner wall mount is a device designed to securely hold and display a television on a wall. It works by attaching the mount to the wall and then the television to the mount using screws or brackets, depending on the type of wall mount. This allows the user to save space and have a more organized setup in their living area. 

There are different types of TV corner wall mounts, including fixed, tilt, swivel, and full-motion mounts. Each type offers a different range of movement and flexibility to accommodate various TV sizes and viewing preferences. The most suitable wall mount for a user will depend on their specific needs and requirements. 


### What materials are commonly used to make TV corner wall mounts?

TV corner wall mounts are typically made from high-strength materials such as steel or aluminum. These materials offer durability, sturdiness, and stability, ensuring that the television remains securely mounted on the wall. Additionally, some wall mounts may include components made of plastic or rubber for enhanced safety and to protect the wall from scratches during installation. 

When selecting a TV corner wall mount, it is essential to consider the material quality and whether it will be able to support the weight of the television without compromising safety or stability. It is also crucial to ensure that the wall mount complies with industry standards and safety regulations. 


### How do I choose the right TV corner wall mount?

To choose the right TV corner wall mount, consider the following factors: the size and weight of your television, the space available on your wall, and your preferred viewing angle and position. Additionally, consider whether you want a fixed, tilt, swivel, or full-motion wall mount, and the range of movement and flexibility each type offers. 

It is also essential to measure the wall where you plan to install the wall mount and ensure that the chosen model is compatible with your television's VESA (Video Electronics Standards Association) mounting hole pattern. Finally, make sure to read product reviews and ratings, as well as compare prices and features, before making a purchase. 


### Do I need professional installation services for a TV corner wall mount?

While professional installation services may not be necessary, they can provide added peace of mind and ensure that your wall mount is securely installed and functioning correctly. Additionally, professionals can help with any additional wiring or cabling requirements and offer advice on the best wall mount for your specific needs. 

If you are comfortable with DIY installation, most wall mounts come with detailed instructions, and many manufacturers offer online resources or customer support to assist with installation. However, if you are unsure or have limited experience with installation, it may be worth considering professional services. 


### How much do TV corner wall mounts typically cost?

The cost of TV corner wall mounts varies depending on the type, size, and features of the mount. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $20 to over $200 for a quality wall mount. Factors that may affect the price include the mount's material, size, and range of movement, as well as any additional features or accessories. 

When shopping for a TV corner wall mount, it is essential to compare prices and features from various manufacturers and retailers to find the best value for your money. Additionally, consider reading product reviews and ratings to ensure that you are investing in a high-quality wall mount that will meet your needs and expectations. 


### Can I wall mount a TV on any type of wall?

While it is possible to mount a TV on most types of walls, certain factors should be considered before selecting a wall for installation. For example, the wall should be strong enough to support the weight of the television without compromising safety or stability. Drywall, concrete, or brick walls are generally suitable for mounting a TV, but it is essential to consult the manufacturer's guidelines and seek professional advice if you are unsure. 

Additionally, the type of wall may affect the installation process. For example, concrete or brick walls may require additional anchoring or specialized mounting hardware to ensure a secure installation. It is also important to ensure that the wall is free from any structural damage or hazards before installing a TV corner wall mount.
