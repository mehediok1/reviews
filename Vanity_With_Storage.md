### What is the difference between a vanity and a pedestal sink?

The main difference between a vanity and a pedestal sink is the storage space offered by each option. A vanity with storage typically includes a sink, countertop, and storage compartments, such as drawers, cabinets, or shelves. These storage compartments provide a convenient and organized space for bathroom essentials. 

A pedestal sink, on the other hand, features a sink and a single pedestal base without storage compartments. While a pedestal sink offers a minimalist and elegant appearance, it lacks the storage space that a vanity with storage provides. Homeowners who require additional storage space for their bathroom essentials may find a vanity with storage to be a more practical and functional option.
