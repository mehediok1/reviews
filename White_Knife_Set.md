### How should I care for and maintain my white knife set?

To ensure that your white knife set maintains its beauty and durability, it is essential to take proper care of it. After each use, hand wash the knives with warm soapy water and gently dry them with a soft cloth. Avoid placing them in dishwashers, as the high temperatures and abrasive detergents can damage the powder coating. For storage, keep the set in a dry place that is protected from sunlight and moisture. This will help prevent rusting and other damage. Storing knives in knife blocks or magnetic strips with a protective sheath on each blade is recommended. Also, ensure to never stack the knives on top of each other, as this can lead to chipping or bending of the blades.
