## FAQ


### Why is unscented cat litter important for cat owners?

Unscented cat litter is an essential product for cat owners because it reduces the strong smell that can come from traditional litter. This improves the odor in the home, leading to a more comfortable environment for both the cats and their owners. It also helps prevent the spread of unwanted smells to other areas of the house, keeping everything fresh and clean. 

Moreover, unscented cat litter is safer for cats with sensitive respiratory systems, ensuring they are not exposed to harsh chemicals or strong fragrances in their litter. This makes it a popular choice for cat owners who want to provide a comfortable and healthy environment for their feline friends. 


### What types of materials are commonly used to make unscented cat litter?

Unscented cat litter can be made from a variety of materials, with some of the most common including clay, silica gel, corn, wheat, and recycled paper. Clay is a popular choice because it is highly absorbent, trapping and containing liquids effectively. Silica gel, on the other hand, is known for its low moisture retention, making it ideal for quick drying and reducing the smell associated with dampness. Additionally, corn, wheat, or recycled paper-based cat litters are eco-friendly options that are widely recognized for their absorbency and odor-control properties. 

It's essential to choose a product that caters to your cat's specific needs and preferences, as well as the characteristics of your litter box and the frequency of cleaning. Research the specific materials used in each product to find the one that best fits your circumstances and ensures a comfortable and healthy environment for your cat. 


### Is unscented cat litter environmentally friendly?

Yes, many unscented cat litters are environmentally friendly. Biodegradable and eco-friendly materials like recycled paper, corn, wheat, and silica gel are used in their production. These materials are renewable, non-toxic, and biodegradable, making them a responsible choice for cat owners who want to reduce their environmental impact while maintaining a clean home. 

In addition, the use of these eco-friendly materials helps reduce waste, as they are made from biodegradable materials that can be broken down and used as a natural resource. This supports a sustainable approach to litter management and contributes to a healthier environment for everyone, including your pets. 


### Can unscented cat litter be used with automatic litter boxes?

Yes, unscented cat litter can be used with automatic litter boxes. Automatic litter boxes are designed to improve litter management, making it more convenient for cat owners by cleaning up after their pets. The type of unscented cat litter you choose can affect the performance of the automatic litter box, so it's essential to read the manufacturer's recommendations and guidelines. Some automatic litter boxes may have specific requirements regarding the size and type of cat litter used. 

For instance, silica gel cat litter is lightweight and can easily be clumped by the rake mechanism of the automatic litter box. Clay litter, on the other hand, may require additional care to ensure it does not stick to the rake, potentially affecting its performance. Ensure that the unscented cat litter you choose is compatible with the automatic litter box you are using to ensure optimal performance and maintain a clean and convenient environment for your cat.
