The aroma profile of a Melissa essential oil is another important factor to consider. A high-quality oil should have a fresh, green, and slightly sweet scent. ### How is Melissa Officinalis Oil made?

The production of Lemon Balm Oil involves the extraction of essential oils from the leaves and flowers of the plant. This process typically uses steam distillation, a method that separates the oil from other components. By collecting and condensing the steam and oil vapors, a pure essential oil is obtained, suitable for diverse applications. 

To guarantee the oil's highest quality and potency, sourcing of plant material from reputable growers and producers is crucial. This ensures that the oil is free from contaminants and adulterants that may compromise its safety and effectiveness.
