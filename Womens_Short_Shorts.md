### Style and Design

When it comes to style, womens short shorts come in a wide variety of designs and colors. Think about the occasions you'll be wearing them and choose accordingly. ### Length and Coverage

Womens short shorts can vary greatly in terms of length and coverage. To find the right fit for you, consider the length of the inseam and the amount of coverage you prefer. Be sure to also consider the coverage of the seat and legs when making your selection. 


### Additional Features

Finally, consider any additional features that may be of interest to you. Some womens short shorts may be lined or have a built-in liner for added comfort and modesty.
