When selecting a tea light holder, consider the material it is made from. Some popular options include glass, ceramic, and metal. Each material has its positives, so the choice ultimately comes down to your personal preference and how it fits into your decor. ### Design

A well-designed tea light holder can elevate the simplicity of a single tealight into a sophisticated decorative element. Look for holders with unique shapes, patterns, or colors that complement your room's aesthetic. You may also want to consider the holder's size and the number of tea lights it can accommodate. ### Safety

Safety should always be a top priority when choosing a tea light holder. Ensure the holder is made from a heat-resistant material and has a flat, stable base. The tea light itself should be securely held in place and any spill-proof design elements are a plus. Furthermore, consider the placement of the holder when lit. It should be away from flammable materials and out of reach of children and pets.

Tea light holders can be formulated from a range of materials, lending each a distinctive appearance and feel. Glass tea light holders possess several advantages, including a timeless and adaptable appearance. ### How long do tea light candles typically burn for?

Standard tea light candles usually burn for 2-4 hours, depending on their size and the type of wax used. Smaller, thinner candles tend to burn faster than larger, thicker ones. The lifespan of the candle can also vary based on the brand or type of wax utilized. It's recommended to discard a tea light candle once it has fully burned out to prevent potential fire hazards. 

When using tea light holders, it's essential to remember not to leave the candle unattended for extended periods. The holder should be positioned on a level surface, away from flammable items. For safety, it's advisable to trim any excess wax from the base of the candle before lighting it.
