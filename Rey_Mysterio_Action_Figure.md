### Scalability

One of the most crucial aspects of an action figure is its scalability. Action figures that are compatible with other figures in your collection are more likely to have better play value and be a more versatile addition to your collection. Look for figures that are part of the same line or have a similar scale to ensure compatibility. 


### Articulation

Articulation is another important factor to consider when purchasing an action figure. Action figures with more articulation points typically have better posability and allow for more creative play scenarios. Look for figures with ball-and-socket joints, swivel joints, and hinge joints to ensure a wide range of motion. Some action figures may also come with special features, like cloth or soft plastic elements that enhance the figure's overall appearance and durability. 


### Accessories

In addition to the figure itself, accessories can also play a significant role in determining the overall quality of an action figure. Look for figures that come with multiple accessories, such as additional hands, weapons, or other gear that can help you recreate scenes from the movies or TV show. Some figures may also come with display stands, which can be a convenient way to show off your action figure collection and make your figures more visually appealing. 


### Brand Reputation

Finally, the brand behind the action figure is also an important factor to consider. Research the brand's reputation for quality, customer service, and attention to detail. Opt for brands that have a strong track record of producing high-quality action figures, as these brands are more likely to deliver a product that meets or exceeds your expectations.
