1. Oval or Round Face: Choose a rectangle frame with bold, angular features to balance the softness of your face. 2. Square or Diamond Face: Opt for frames with softer, rounded edges to balance out your angular features. 4. Triangle Face: Choose a frame with a top-heavy style or a wider upper portion to counterbalance the narrower bottom portion of your face. 5. Heart-shaped Face: Select frames with heavier bottoms or a unique color scheme to help balance out the upper portion of your face.

### How should I measure my face to find the perfect rectangle glasses frame?

This measurement will ensure that the lenses provide optimal vision and comfort.
