### Storing and Shelf Life of Charcuterie Crackers

Charcuterie crackers are best enjoyed within a few days of opening and should be stored in an airtight container to ensure their freshness. Proper storage conditions can typically result in a shelf life of about a week. 

For those seeking to prolong the shelf life, freezing the crackers in an airtight container for up to three months can help maintain their quality, though it is always a good idea to check their texture before consuming.
