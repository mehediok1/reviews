## FAQ


### What is Whey Protein?

Whey protein is a high-quality protein derived from milk. It is a byproduct of cheese production and contains all the essential amino acids that the human body needs. Whey protein is easily digestible, making it an excellent choice for building muscle and supporting overall health. It is also low in fat and carbohydrates, making it suitable for those on a low-calorie diet. 

Clean whey protein is a type of whey protein that is produced using high-quality, grass-fed milk. It undergoes cold filtration and low-heat drying processes, ensuring that it retains all the essential nutrients found in the whey protein. This process minimizes the risk of chemical contamination, giving you a clean and pure protein source.

Clean whey protein offers numerous benefits for both fitness enthusiasts and those seeking to maintain their overall health. It helps in muscle recovery, promoting muscle growth, and aids in weight management. It also works as an excellent appetite suppressant, making it beneficial for those trying to lose weight. Moreover, due to the high-quality production process, clean whey protein is free from harmful additives and contaminants. Overall, incorporating clean whey protein into your daily routine can have a positive impact on your health and fitness journey.
