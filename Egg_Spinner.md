## FAQ


### What is an Egg Spinner?

An Egg Spinner is a kitchen tool designed to effortlessly separate the egg white from the yolk with minimal mess. It consists of a hollow cylinder that fits over the top of a bowl, and a spinning handle that spins when the egg is cracked into the device. The spinning motion separates the yolk from the white as the egg is cracked, making it easy to collect and discard the yolk. 

Some egg spinners are designed with additional features, such as a built-in yolk catcher, which makes the process even more convenient. These tools are particularly useful for those who frequently cook recipes that require just egg whites or enjoy making meringues or other egg white-dependent dishes. 


### What makes an Egg Spinner different from a regular bowl and fork technique?

An Egg Spinner offers several advantages over the traditional method of separating egg whites from yolks using a bowl and fork. Firstly, it ensures that the yolk is fully separated from the egg white, which can be difficult to achieve using a fork. Additionally, using a fork increases the chance of contaminating the egg white with yolk, while the spinning motion of the Egg Spinner eliminates this risk. 

Moreover, an Egg Spinner offers a more hygienic and mess-free approach. Since the yolk is collected in a separate container, it avoids the need to handle it with your fingers or transfer it to a separate container, reducing the likelihood of cross-contamination or spills. Overall, an Egg Spinner simplifies and streamlines the process of separating egg whites from yolks.
