## FAQ


### What is an Ice Ball?

An Ice Ball, also known as a large spherical ice cube, is a popular type of ice used for making cocktails and various drinks. This unique shape captures the attention of drinkers and adds a certain 'Wow' factor to any beverage. The Ice Ball also melts slowly, which means it chills drinks without watering them down. 

The Ice Ball is typically made from a mold which creates it in a perfect sphere shape. It's made from filtered water and can be flavored for extra taste. The process of making them is simple and doesn't require any advanced equipment, just a special mold and a freezer. The ice is usually made in quantities to be used for the day, as they need to be made and served immediately for the perfect effect. 


### What are the benefits of using an Ice Ball?

Ice Balls have a few key benefits compared to standard ice cubes. They melt very slowly, which means your drink will stay colder for longer. Unlike ice cubes, they don't dilute the drink as much because of their larger size. This makes them an excellent choice for cocktails, as they add an impressive aesthetic to the glass while keeping the drink perfectly chilled. 

Another advantage is that they're less harsh on the palate. Regular ice cubes are made of small pieces which can be hard and uncomfortable to bite or chew. With an Ice Ball, your drink is chilled without having to deal with the discomfort of small, hard pieces of ice. All these factors contribute to the overall experience and enjoyment of your beverage. And of course, their aesthetic appeal adds an extra touch of elegance to any drink. 


### How do I make an Ice Ball?

Making an Ice Ball is a fairly simple process. First, you'll need a specialized mold for making spheres. Fill this mold partially with water and place it in the freezer. Depending on the size of your mold, the Ice Ball should be ready by morning. It's important to note that once the Ice Ball is made, it should be used immediately to enjoy it at its best. 

You can add flavor to your Ice Ball if you wish. Some common choices include lemon or lime juice, mint leaves, or even the zest of a fruit such as orange. Simply place your preferred flavoring in the mold before filling it with water. Once the Ice Ball is frozen, it can be removed from the mold and enjoyed in any beverage of your choice. With practice, you'll be able to make perfect Ice Balls every time. 


### What kind of mold is used to make Ice Balls?

There are various kinds of molds available in the market for making Ice Balls. They range from simple plastic molds to more intricate stainless steel models. You can choose one based on your preference and budget. The basic requirement for an ice mold is that it needs to create a perfectly spherical shape. Some molds are designed for a specific size of Ice Ball, so make sure to look at the details before making a purchase. 

Additionally, there are reusable molds available where you fill the mold, freeze the water, and then take out the Ice Ball. This saves you the hassle of buying a new mold every time you want to make an Ice Ball. These molds are usually made of materials like silicone or stainless steel, known for their durability and ease of cleaning. Remember to clean your mold after each use before storing it away. 


### What is the best way to store Ice Balls?

The best way to store Ice Balls is to keep them in the freezer. Since they're made for immediate use, it's not advisable to store them at room temperature as they will start to melt. It's also important not to store them in a sealed container as this could lead to the formation of ice crystals, affecting the quality and taste of the Ice Ball. Storing them loose in the freezer allows air to circulate around them, which helps maintain their perfect spherical shape. 

If you're planning on using Ice Balls for a larger event, you can make them in advance and keep them in the freezer until the day of the event. Just remember to plan your storage space in the freezer accordingly. Lastly, avoid stacking or piling up the Ice Balls in the freezer as this can damage their perfect spherical shape. Store them on a flat surface or in a container that can hold their shape. This will ensure they're ready to use whenever you need them. 


### Can I flavor Ice Balls?

Yes, you can flavor Ice Balls. Making flavored Ice Balls can elevate your cocktails to a new level of enjoyment. The most common way to flavor Ice Balls is to add flavorings like mint, basil, rosemary or citrus zest to the water before filling the mold. Alternatively, you can soak the Ice Ball in a liquid that you want to infuse it with, such as a syrup, liqueur, or even tea. The flavor will be absorbed slowly into the ice as it melts, adding an extra layer of taste to your drink. 

Another way to flavor Ice Balls is by dipping them in alcohol or other liquid before serving. If you want to use this method, ensure that you wash the surface of the Ice Ball with alcohol to prevent any water from mixing with the liquid. This can be a good option if you want to use different flavors for each Ice Ball to cater to different tastes. Remember, when flavoring Ice Balls, it's all about experimentation. Feel free to mix and match different ingredients to find the perfect combination for you.
